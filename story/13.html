DATA ANALYSIS

The Composition of Clusters

In 2020, there are significantly more stations belonging to cluster 1 (two-peaks pattern) but less stations classified to clusters 3 (PM-peak pattern), indicating that the impact of the COVID-19 on London underground travel patterns is mainly reflected in the increase in two-peak patterns (cluster 1) and the decrease in PM-peak patterns (cluster 3).

The reason for this shift may be the implementation of remote working and learning policy and several major lockdowns, which has reduced the demand for unnecessary commuting, especially for recreation and shopping in the afternoon or in the evening. The change of numbers of stations in cluster 1 mainly occurs on a weekday (the proportion of MTT and FRI increased from 74% to 93%), which further supports this conclusion.
