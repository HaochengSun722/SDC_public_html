Impacts on Journey Patterns at Station Level

Based on the Annual Station Counts dataset (provided by TfL), we adopted the dynamic time warping (DTW) clustering algorithm to investigate different temporal profiles of travel patterns on the London Underground and the impact of COVID-19 on it.
