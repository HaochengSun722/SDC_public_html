Changes in Hourly/Daily Pattern

during the pandemic, journeys in every 15 minutes during a typical day have all significantly decreased by 2/3 to 3/4. Yet, the overall pattern of traffic fluctuations during a day did not change, and the two peaks still exist.

Journey Change in a Week before and during the pandemic shows a similar trend.

Before the pandemic, weekend journeys were about half of the weekday's counterparts. During the pandemic, on the one hand, overall metro journeys dropped to a quarter of the former state; On the other hand, weekend ourneys witnessed a greater decrease, accounting for only 1/3 of weekday journeys.
