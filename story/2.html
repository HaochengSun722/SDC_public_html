INTRODUCTION

After the outbreak of the pandemic, public transport journeys were severely affected by the soaring number of cases; from Feb 2020 to May 2020, and from Oct 2020 to Feb 2021, there were 2 cliff-like drops in public traffic journeys, which coincided with London's 2 major lockdowns.

Overall, London Underground together with other modes of public transport have been severely impacted twice by the epidemic, and the journeys of all modes of public transport basically kept changing at a synchronized pace during the epidemic. However, The proportion of those pubilc transports before and after the epidemic remains basically stable.

After dipping into the overall trend of the London Underground along with various modes of public transport, let's take a closer look at the specific changes in London Underground traffic before and after the outbreak.
