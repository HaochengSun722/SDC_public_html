Decline of Journeys in Tube Lines

According to the official TFL website, there are 15 tube lines. We calculated the sum of the changes in the number of journeys for all stations on each line.

As seen in the line charts below, before and after the pandemic, the number of journeys on different routes all witnessed varying degrees of decline. Some tube lines suffered from a smaller drop of 2/3 of the original number of journeys like DLR, and some experienced a larger drop to less than 1/7 of its original like 'Waterloo and City.' Besides, there are some lines where the morning and evening peaks are less pronounced, such as 'Central' and 'Hammersmith and City, but there are also lines where the AM and PM peaks remain noticeable, such as 'Overground' and 'DLR.'We speculate that the morning and evening peaks are still maintained at lines that connect more concentrated residential areas and employment.
