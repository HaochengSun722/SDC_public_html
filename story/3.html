Data Source & Spatial Scale

According to the London Underground annual counts[1] and the TFL website, there are 16 tube lines and 426 stations distinguished by name, among which 392 are within the borough boundaries of the 33 boroughs. According to the cumulative COVID-19 cases, as of 28 April, there are 719,190 confirmed covid-19 cases, with Newham, Redbridge, Ealing, Croydon, and Barnet reporting the highest numbers of cases among different boroughs.
