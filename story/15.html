CASE STUDY

Brentwood

The overall size of the flow decreased significantly by around 70%. In terms of the temporal profiles, it can be seen that the travel patterns of Brentwood on Friday transformed from cluster 2, and 3 to cluster 1.

According to the first figure, there was a significant AM peak at around 7-8 AM (consistent with our description of cluster 2) as well as a significant PM peak at around 5-6 PM (consistent with our description of cluster 3), which indicates that many people go to work in the morning at this station and come back after getting off work in the evening. While these two temporal profiles changed to two-peaks patterns after COVID-19, indicating the switch of the most common travel behavior which can be explained by the implementation of remote working policy. However, travel patterns on Sunday did not meet a significant change.
