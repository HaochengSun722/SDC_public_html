DATA ANALYSIS

Decline of Journeys in Boroughs

In addition to the graphical representation, statistical analysis was also adopted. Linear regression was processed on the cumulative number of reported cases and the decline in the London Underground journeys for each borough. Another attempt was made to fit a linear gregression after taking the logs for the positively skewed variables. But as the graph shows, whether taking logs or not, the relationship between them was not significant either way, with shallow R2 values and high p-values.
