DATA ANALYSIS

Changes in Hourly/Daily Pattern

When comparing different change patterns between weekdays and weekends, journeys on weekdays dropped to 1/4 of the original number, while the trips at weekends further dropped to 1/6, which is to say, the decline on weekdays is smaller. Meanwhile, after the pandemic, the two peaks at weekends are much flatter and smoother and almost invisible.

In contrast, peaks on workdays are still apparent, suggesting that the pandemic had a more significant impact on leisure and non-essential journeys.
