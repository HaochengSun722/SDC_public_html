PREFACE


Hello! Everyone! Welcome to the ‘Interactive Map of the Impact of Covid-19 on the London Tube System’!

In this map, you will use a series of interactive measures to identify the changes in the London tube system before and during the impact of Covid-19. The operation path can basically be divided into:

Basic mode selection → Select the station you want to observe → Understand the detailed information

If you already understand the process, please click the "SKIP" button; if you are browsing this website for the first time, please click the "NEXT" button!


Let's explore the story of the London tube system behind Covid-19 together!

Step 1:
Let us select a year before or during the pandemic.

Step 2:
Here you can select the inbound or outbound flow for all stations.

Step 3:
Then you can select a tipical day in a week. Here, Monday to Thursday are combined into one type.

Step 4:
Finally, just select a specific time period. When you confirm, the time you selected will be displayed in the navigation bar.

Don't forget that you need to confirm every time you change the modes.
If you forget the above, you can view the specific operation by clicking on a different logo.
